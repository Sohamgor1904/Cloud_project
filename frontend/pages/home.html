Platform Features

Everything you need to
run smarter collections

Built on a three-tier cloud architecture — HTML/CSS/JS frontend, Node.js Express backend, and PostgreSQL database.

🗺

Live GIFT City Map

22 bin markers colour-coded by fill level across 4 zone boundaries on a Leaflet.js dark map. Dashed route polyline. Click any marker to inspect.

Leaflet.jsCartoDB DarkHaversine routing
🧠

Priority Scoring

priority = (fill% × 0.1) + noise(0–2)
Bins ≥ 7.0 enter the route. Top 10 dispatched to driver.

🚛

Greedy Route Optimiser

60% fill urgency + 40% travel proximity. Real Haversine distances. Prevents backtracking across the city.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

Chart.js doughnut (fill distribution), horizontal bar (top-10 priorities), zone-average charts. Live on every simulation.

🔮

Overflow Predictions

AI-modelled fill rates predict overflow time — 2h, 4h, 8h projections. Alert engine fires at 75% and 90% fill.

System Flow

From sensor to dispatch in seconds

Simulate IoT Sensors

2

Priority Calculation

fill% × 0.1 + urgency(0–2). Bins ≥ 7.0 qualify for today's route.

3

Persist to Database

Written to fill_history in PostgreSQL. Route reads from DB — never stale data.

4

Route Optimisation

Greedy algorithm: 0.6 × fill − 0.4 × distance. Truck starts at depot.

Map Dispatch

City map updates with ranked stops, polyline, and overflow alerts. Driver follows this order.
